About Us

At Little Heart Pet Shop, we believe every pet deserves to feel pampered, loved, and cared for—just like family. With a blend of grooming expertise and love for animals, we work with clean aesthetics, calm vibes, and nurturing environment for your furry companions.

Our lead groomer is a Certified Professional Groomer from the Australian Dog Grooming School and has years of experience in leading Australian grooming salons. At Little Heart, we focus on personalized care and one-on-one pet sessions, where we adapt each grooming session to not only address appearance but also comfort, health and peace of mind.

We use premium grooming tools and high-quality products to deliver salon-standard results with a personal touch. From their first haircut to their routine care, at our grooming sessions, we tailor each session to your pet’s individual needs.

Whether it’s their first bath or their regular tidy-up, your pet is in expert hands.
Your pet’s comfort leads the way in our journey.
